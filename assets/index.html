Sound fragrance for your rainy days.

雨の日に彩りを添えるミュージックブランド。2019年より東京恵比寿を拠点に活動。

R0M.TOKYO is electric project from Tokyo, Japan.
our sound is influenced by future-garage, Wave, Shoegaze, post-rock and post-classical, and is complicated by their crossovers. We has been working to update the aging Tokyo scene.
